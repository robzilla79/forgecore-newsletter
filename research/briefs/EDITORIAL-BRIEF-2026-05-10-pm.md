### Workflow/Code Idea
1. **Identify Routine Tasks**: List common client communication tasks (e.g., follow-ups, scheduling).
2. **Set Up Triggers**: Use Zapier to create triggers for when a client emails or fills out a form.
3. **Automate Responses**: Create automated responses using templates for common inquiries.
4. **Integrate with Calendar**: Sync with your calendar to automatically schedule meetings based on client availability.
5. **Monitor and Adjust**: Regularly review automated interactions to ensure quality and make adjustments as necessary.
