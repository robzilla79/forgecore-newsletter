## How to Use AEO Competitor Analysis to Boost Your Brand's Visibility in AI Responses

### Target Operator
Marketing Manager at a mid-sized company looking to enhance brand visibility and optimize content strategy.

### Job-to-be-Done
Implement a systematic approach to track and analyze competitor visibility in AI-generated answers, enabling informed content optimization.

### Reader Outcome
Increase brand visibility in AI-generated responses by 30% within three months.

### Thesis
A marketing manager can leverage AEO competitor analysis to identify content gaps and optimize their strategy, resulting in improved brand visibility in AI-generated answers.

### Why Now
As AI-generated content increasingly shapes consumer perceptions, brands that do not adapt their strategies risk losing visibility. Recent insights from HubSpot highlight the importance of AEO competitor analysis in understanding how brands are cited in AI responses, making it essential for marketers to act now to remain competitive. [Source](https://blog.hubspot.com/marketing/aeo-competitor-analysis).

### Tool Stack
1. **SEMrush** - For identifying competitors and tracking keyword performance.
2. **Ahrefs** - To analyze competitor content and backlink profiles.
3. **Google Search Console** - For monitoring site performance and visibility.
4. **AEO Tracking Tools** (e.g., AnswerThePublic) - To track how often your brand is cited in AI-generated answers.

### Monetization Fit
No forced monetization; however, affiliate links to SEMrush and Ahrefs can be included as they provide valuable insights for the reader's workflow.

### Workflow
1. **Identify Competitors**: Use SEMrush or Ahrefs to find out which brands are being cited in AI-generated answers for your target keywords.
2. **Analyze Competitor Content**: Review the top-performing content of these competitors to understand what makes it appealing to AI engines.
3. **Optimize Your Content**: Adjust your content strategy based on insights gained from competitor analysis, focusing on areas where you can improve or fill gaps.
4. **Set Up AEO Tracking**: Implement AEO prompt tracking tools to monitor your brand's visibility in AI responses over time.
5. **Iterate and Improve**: Regularly revisit your analysis and tracking to refine your strategy and stay competitive.

### Tradeoffs
- **Cost**: Subscription fees for tools like SEMrush and Ahrefs can add up.
- **Learning Curve**: Familiarizing yourself with new tools and methodologies may take time.
- **Speed**: Implementing changes based on insights may take time to reflect in AI responses.
