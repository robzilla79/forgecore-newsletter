## Job-to-be-Done

Solo creators can use AI tools to efficiently repurpose long-form video content into engaging short-form clips, increasing audience engagement and potential revenue.

With the rise of short-form video platforms like TikTok and Instagram Reels, creators need to adapt their content strategies to capture audience attention quickly. According to a recent report by HubSpot, 54% of consumers want to see more video content from brands they support, making it crucial for creators to leverage their existing content effectively.

## Tool Stack
1. **OpusClip** - for automatic video clipping and editing.
2. **Descript** - for transcript-based editing and audio/video production.
3. **Canva Pro** - for creating engaging thumbnails and social media graphics.
4. **Repurpose.io** - for automating the distribution of clips across multiple platforms.

## Workflow
1. **Select Long-Form Content**: Choose a recent webinar, podcast, or video that has valuable insights.
2. **Use OpusClip**: Upload the video to OpusClip to automatically generate short clips based on key moments.
3. **Edit with Descript**: Refine the clips using Descript for any necessary audio or visual adjustments.
4. **Create Thumbnails in Canva**: Design eye-catching thumbnails for each clip to enhance click-through rates on social media.
5. **Schedule Distribution with Repurpose.io**: Set up automated posting of the clips across your social media channels to maximize reach.

## Tradeoffs
- **Cost**: Subscription fees for tools like OpusClip and Repurpose.io can add up, but they save time in the long run.
- **Learning Curve**: Familiarizing yourself with multiple tools may take time, but the efficiency gained is worth the investment.
- **Quality vs. Speed**: Automated tools may not always produce perfect results, requiring manual adjustments for high-stakes content.
