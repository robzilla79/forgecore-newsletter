### Workflow/Code Idea
1. **Select Existing Content**: Choose a recorded webinar or podcast episode.
2. **Use Castmagic**: Upload the recording to Castmagic to generate transcripts and summaries.
3. **Repurpose Content**: Create blog posts, social media snippets, and newsletters from the generated content.
4. **Use OpusClip**: Upload the long-form video to OpusClip to create short clips for social media.
5. **Schedule Distribution**: Use a content calendar to plan and schedule the distribution of repurposed content across platforms.
6. **Analyze Engagement**: Monitor engagement metrics to assess the effectiveness of the repurposed content.
