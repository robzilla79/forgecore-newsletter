## Tool Stack
1. **Otter.ai** - for real-time transcription of meetings.
2. **Descript** - for editing and summarizing transcriptions.
3. **Notion** - for organizing notes and action items.
4. **Zapier** - for automating workflows between tools.

## Workflow
1. **Set Up Otter.ai**: Create an account and integrate it with your calendar to automatically join and record meetings.
2. **Transcribe Meetings**: Use Otter.ai to transcribe your client meetings in real-time.
3. **Edit Transcriptions**: After the meeting, use Descript to clean up the transcription, removing filler words and irrelevant sections.
4. **Summarize Key Points**: Create a summary of action items and important notes directly in Descript or export to Notion.
5. **Automate Follow-Ups**: Use Zapier to send follow-up emails based on the summarized notes, ensuring timely communication with clients.
6. **Review and Adjust**: Regularly assess the effectiveness of your transcriptions and follow-up processes to make necessary adjustments.

## Tradeoffs
- **Cost**: Subscription fees for tools like Otter.ai and Descript can add up, but they may save time and increase productivity.
- **Privacy**: Using cloud-based transcription services may raise concerns about client confidentiality.
- **Learning Curve**: Familiarizing yourself with multiple tools may require time and effort.
- **Quality**: Automated transcriptions may not be 100% accurate, necessitating manual review.
