## Ranked Story Angles

1. **How Solo Founders Can Use AI to Optimize Pricing Strategies for Digital Products**
   - **Operator persona:** Solo founders in the digital product space.
   - **Job-to-be-done:** Establishing competitive pricing for digital products based on market analysis and customer behavior.
   - **Measurable outcome:** Potentially increase revenue by 15% through optimized pricing strategies.
   - **Tool stack:** AI pricing tools like PriceIntelligence, Google Trends for market analysis, and Excel for data manipulation.
   - **Monetization fit:** Natural fit for affiliate tools that assist in pricing strategy.
   - **Why now:** Recent advancements in AI analytics tools allow for more nuanced pricing strategies that adapt to real-time market conditions.
   - **Rejected framing:** Avoiding a generic overview of pricing strategies without specific AI applications.

3. **Leveraging AI for Efficient Market Research in Product Development**
   - **Operator persona:** Indie hackers and small business operators developing new products.
   - **Job-to-be-done:** Conducting market research to inform product development decisions.
   - **Measurable outcome:** Save up to 10 hours per week on research tasks.
   - **Tool stack:** AI-driven market research tools like SurveyMonkey, Google Trends, and social media analytics.

   - **Rejected framing:** Avoiding a generic overview of market research without specific AI applications.

4. **Automating Customer Feedback Collection with AI Tools**
   - **Operator persona:** Coaches and consultants who rely on client feedback for service improvement.
   - **Job-to-be-done:** Streamlining the process of collecting and analyzing customer feedback.
   - **Measurable outcome:** Save 5 hours per week on feedback collection and analysis.
   - **Tool stack:** AI feedback tools like Typeform, Google Forms, and sentiment analysis software.
   - **Monetization fit:** Potential for affiliate partnerships with feedback tools.
   - **Why now:** The demand for real-time feedback has increased, and AI tools can automate this process effectively.
   - **Rejected framing:** Avoiding a broad discussion on feedback collection without actionable AI insights.
