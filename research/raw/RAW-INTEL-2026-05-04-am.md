## High-Signal Intel Memo

### What Changed
The emergence of AI answer engines like ChatGPT and Google's AI Overviews has transformed how brands are perceived in search results. Instead of traditional rankings, these engines cite sources directly, meaning that companies can be overlooked even if they rank highly in organic search. This shift necessitates a new approach to competitor analysis, specifically focusing on who is being cited in AI-generated answers.

### Why It Matters
For solo operators, understanding which competitors are being cited in AI answers is crucial for making informed content and SEO decisions. This knowledge can help them refine their strategies to ensure they are included in these AI-generated responses, ultimately driving more traffic and potential revenue.

### Source-Backed Notes
- **AEO Competitor Analysis**: A method to track which competitors are cited by AI answer engines for specific queries.
- **Impact on SEO**: Traditional SEO metrics may no longer provide a complete picture of visibility and competition.
- **Actionable Insights**: Operators can leverage this analysis to adjust their content strategies and improve their chances of being cited.
