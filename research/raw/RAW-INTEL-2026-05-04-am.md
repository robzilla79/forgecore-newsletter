## High-Signal Intel Memo

### What Changed
The emergence of AI answer engines like ChatGPT and Google's AI Overviews has transformed how brands are perceived in search results. Instead of traditional rankings, these engines cite sources directly, meaning that companies can be overlooked even if they rank highly in organic search. This shift necessitates a new approach to competitor analysis, focusing on who gets cited in AI-generated answers and why.

### Why It Matters
For solo operators and small business owners, understanding this new landscape is crucial. If competitors are being cited in AI answers while you are not, you risk losing visibility and potential customers. Implementing an AEO competitor analysis can help identify gaps in your content strategy and inform your marketing decisions, ultimately leading to increased traffic and revenue.
