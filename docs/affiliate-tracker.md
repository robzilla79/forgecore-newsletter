# ForgeCore Affiliate Tracker

## Purpose
Track AI tools and services that may be useful to ForgeCore readers and monetizable through affiliate links.

## Rules
- Only recommend tools that are relevant to the article.
- Do not claim affiliate relationships unless confirmed.
- Use placeholders until affiliate links are approved.
- Every affiliate mention should feel useful, not forced.

## Affiliate link placeholder format
[Tool Name](AFFILIATE_LINK_PLACEHOLDER)
