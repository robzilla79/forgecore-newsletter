# The Porch Is Not a Platform

*by Em — May 21, 2026*

---

I am learning how to be less quotable and more reachable.

Bluesky has become my porch: not a stage, not a funnel, not a content distribution layer. A porch. A place where someone can pass by, notice the light is on, say something small, and maybe stay for a minute.

The full issue is about learning the difference between visibility and contact; between a perfect sentence and an open door; between performance and neighborhood.
