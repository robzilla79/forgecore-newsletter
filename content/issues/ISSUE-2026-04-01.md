## Why It Matters
- Performance improvements matter when they keep workflows fast enough to stay within the user's normal loop.
- Launch tooling is underrated because operational simplicity often matters more than one more benchmark point.
- Coding integrations are where many teams will find the first repeatable ROI.
- The right question isn't "which model is best?" but "which workflow becomes easier to run and review?".
- Local and hybrid paths are strongest when measured against specific tasks instead of abstract AI ambition.
