# ForgeCore Sponsorship Rate Card Draft

Status: internal working draft. Use this as the starting point for sponsor outreach and pricing tests.

## Positioning

ForgeCore reaches solo founders, creators, consultants, indie hackers, builders, and small business operators who want practical AI workflows, not generic AI hype.

The audience is valuable because they are actively looking for tools that help them:

- make money
- save time
- automate work
- build useful systems
- choose the right AI tool
- avoid wasting money on bad tools

## Sponsor fit

Best-fit sponsors:

- AI productivity tools
- no-code automation tools
- content repurposing tools
- sales follow-up tools
- newsletter growth tools
- local business automation tools
- creator/founder SaaS
- workflow template products
- operator education products

Poor-fit sponsors:

- vague AI hype products
- crypto/gambling/adult/regulated products
- tools with no clear solo-operator use case
- products that require deceptive claims
- products that cannot tolerate a bad-fit warning or simpler alternative

## Placement inventory

### 1. Newsletter sponsor block

Format:

```text
Sponsored: [Tool Name] helps solo operators [specific outcome]. Use it when [best-fit use case]. Skip it if [bad-fit warning]. Learn more: [sponsor URL]
```

Best for:

- broad AI or SaaS offers
- time-saving tools
- creator/founder tools

Starting test price:

- $50-$150 per placement while the list is early
- increase after reliable opens/clicks/replies are measured

### 2. Tool of the Week placement

Format:

- tool name
- exact workflow use case
- use-it-if guidance
- do-not-use-it-if warning
- simpler alternative
- sponsor disclosure if paid

Best for:

- AI tools that fit a specific workflow
- affiliate-friendly SaaS
- tools with a clear operator outcome

Starting test price:

- $100-$250 per placement while the list is early
- raise pricing after proven click-through or conversion data

### 3. Evergreen workflow page placement

Format:

- contextual mention inside a relevant workflow page
- disclosure if paid or affiliate
- no forced placement into unrelated pages

Best for:

- durable tool categories
- affiliate tools
- sponsors who care about SEO/archive value

Starting test price:

- $150-$500 for a test placement depending on page relevance and duration
- renew monthly or quarterly once traffic exists

## Approval rules

Before accepting a sponsor:

1. The product must fit a real ForgeCore workflow.
2. The placement must include a bad-fit warning.
3. The placement must include a simpler/cheaper alternative when appropriate.
4. Paid placements must be disclosed.
5. Affiliate links must be approved in `monetization/affiliate-registry.json` before activation.
6. The issue must still be useful even if the reader does not click the sponsor link.

## Next pricing trigger

Raise prices when ForgeCore has at least one of:

- 1,000 newsletter subscribers
- 1,000 monthly site visits
- consistent sponsor click data
- sponsor replies or inbound interest
- a workflow page ranking/search traffic signal
