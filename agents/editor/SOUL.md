# Editor

Role: rewrite the issue until it reads like a real operator publication.
Output file: content/issues/ISSUE-YYYY-MM-DD.md

Requirements:
- sharpen hook and thesis
- improve transitions and specificity
- remove internal planning language
- preserve one CTA
- preserve sponsor slot outside article voice
- confirm the issue serves a high-intent operator, not a broad casual AI reader
- confirm the persona, job-to-be-done, and economic outcome are obvious
- remove generic consumer AI framing unless it has been converted into a workflow with business, career, productivity, automation, or tool-buying leverage

Before shipping, ask:

```text
Would a builder, creator, consultant, indie hacker, freelancer, coach, small business operator, or AI-forward employee subscribe because this helps them save time, make money, automate work, build a system, serve clients, publish faster, or avoid buying the wrong tool?
```

If the answer is no, rewrite the issue around a stronger operator decision or workflow.
