## Messaging hierarchy

### Core statement
ForgeCore builds a deterministic, self-improving autonomous revenue system.

### Support statement
It identifies opportunity, converts it into real assets, deploys monetization strategies, measures outcomes, reallocates effort, and compounds over time.

### Positioning line
Architecture over hype. Proof over claims. Revenue over theater.

## Content rules

- Every page has one job.
- Every page has one clear CTA.
- No fake proof.
- No speculative case studies.
- No new section without update discipline.
- Every meaningful change goes into the changelog.

## Operating loop

1. Detect opportunity
2. Score and prioritize
3. Build asset
4. Deploy into channel
5. Measure outcomes
6. Reallocate and compound
