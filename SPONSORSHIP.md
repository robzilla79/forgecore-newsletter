# Sponsor ForgeCore

ForgeCore is an AI tools newsletter and website for solo operators, creators, builders, indie hackers, consultants, and small business operators.

ForgeCore helps readers use AI to save time, automate work, build practical systems, choose better tools, and avoid wasting money on bad software.

The audience does not want generic AI hype. They want workflows they can use this week.

## Audience

ForgeCore is built for:

- solo founders
- creators
- indie hackers
- consultants
- small business operators
- builders using AI to ship faster
- operators who want practical systems, not trend-chasing

Readers are looking for tools and workflows that help them:

- make money
- save time
- automate repeatable work
- publish more consistently
- research faster
- build internal systems
- improve marketing and sales operations
- choose the right AI tools before spending money

## Sponsor Fit

ForgeCore is a strong fit for companies selling tools in these categories:

- AI productivity

## Available Placements

Potential sponsor placements include:

1. **Newsletter sponsor block**
   A short sponsor placement inside a ForgeCore issue.

2. **Tool of the Week feature**
   A workflow-driven tool spotlight when the product fits the issue topic.

3. **Website article placement**
   A disclosure-backed mention inside a relevant article or workflow.

4. **Dedicated workflow teardown**
   A practical tutorial showing how a reader would use the product to complete a specific job.

5. **Lead magnet or blueprint sponsorship**
   Sponsorship attached to a downloadable workflow, checklist, or operating system.

## Editorial Trust Policy

ForgeCore monetization must help the reader first.

Sponsor and affiliate placements must follow these rules:

- The tool must be tied to a real workflow or job-to-be-done.
- The issue should explain who the tool is best for.
- The issue should include a bad-fit warning when a tool is not appropriate.
- Sponsored or affiliate placements must be disclosed clearly.
- ForgeCore should not recommend a tool only because it pays.
- ForgeCore should not turn useful articles into link farms.
- Free, cheaper, or simpler alternatives should be mentioned when useful.
